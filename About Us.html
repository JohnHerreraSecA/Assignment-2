A Brief History of New Republik Barbershop

New Republik Barbershop began its journey in the heart of South Etobicoke, founded by two childhood friends with a shared passion for the art of barbering. During the challenging times of the pandemic, these friends saw an opportunity to provide quality haircuts and grooming services to their community. What started as a humble endeavor in a small garage quickly garnered a loyal clientele, thanks to their exceptional skills and dedication.

As word spread about their craftsmanship and friendly service, New Republik Barbershop grew beyond its modest beginnings. The duo's commitment to excellence and their vision of creating a welcoming space for everyone fueled their expansion. Today, New Republik Barbershop stands as a testament to their hard work and perseverance, offering a wide range of services while maintaining the personal touch that started it all.

Conveniently located at Humber College, North Campus in Etobicoke, Ontario, New Republic Barbershop offers a blend of modern style and traditional craftsmanship. Our dedicated team provides expert haircuts, beard trims, and grooming services in a welcoming atmosphere.

Operating hours: Monday to Friday: 10:00am - 6:00pm Saturday and Sunday: 12:00pm - 5:00pm
